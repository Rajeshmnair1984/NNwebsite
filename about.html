Who We Are

Neighborhood roots.
Enterprise expertise.

Near Nerd's journey began in 2023 as We-Tech, a focused cellphone repair and telecommunications company built on over 7 years of experience as an authorized Bell Telecommunications dealership.

In 2025, following our acquisition, We-Tech transformed into Near Nerd — with an expanded vision to become a comprehensive technology solutions provider while maintaining the personal, community-focused approach that made us special.

Today, with successful locations in Whitehorse, Morinville, and Sylvan Lake, we've proven that combining technical expertise with genuine customer care creates lasting relationships and drives real business success.
