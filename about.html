The Near Nerd Story

Near Nerd's journey began in 2023 as We-Tech, a focused cellphone repair and telecommunications company built on over 7 years of experience as an authorized Bell Telecommunications dealership. In 2025, following our acquisition, We-Tech transformed into Near Nerd with an expanded vision to become a comprehensive technology solutions provider while maintaining the personal, community-focused approach that made us special. Today, with successful locations in Whitehorse, Morinville, and Sylvan Lake, we have proven that combining technical expertise with genuine customer care creates lasting relationships and drives business success. As we expand throughout British Columbia, Alberta, and Ontario, our mission remains unchanged: to be your trusted neighborhood destination where cutting-edge technology solutions meet old-fashioned customer service, helping communities stay connected and making technology accessible to everyone.
